Experimental Design:

Experiment 1: Have AutoGPT Complete a Data Analysis Report

· Objective: Experience the autonomous task planning and execution capabilities of an AI Agent.

· Steps:

1) Use the Titanic dataset and provide the goal: "Please analyze this dataset and generate a report containing key statistical information and visualizations."

2) Start AutoGPT and observe how it plans tasks (e.g., loading data, calculating descriptive statistics, plotting the relationship between survival rate and gender, summarizing findings).

3) Analyze its execution process, documenting its successes and areas where it might make mistakes or get stuck in loops.

Experiment 2: Compare AutoGPT with a Manually Guided Analysis Process

· Objective: Develop a rational understanding of the current capabilities and limitations of AI Agents.

· Steps:

1) For the same analysis task, perform it manually and record the steps and time taken.

2) Compare the report generated by AutoGPT with the manually generated report in terms of depth, accuracy, and efficiency.

3) Discuss in which scenarios AI Agents can genuinely improve efficiency and what the main current challenges are.
